All About Charkz!


Wojtek ‘Charkz’ Hordyniec is a house DJ based in Dublin, Ireland. Known for sending rooms into a state of groovy dance, most known for playing groovy and melodic house songs, working of dance music, and disco house, heavily influenced by Charkz’ love for funk music. Charkz was born in Kozalin, Poland, at a young age both his parents decided to move to Donegal, Ireland. Charkz’ young life was spent playing video games and listening to any music his sister was listening too. At the time it was a heavy amount of indie rock. rock , and punk rock. Over time Charkz’ taste moved into funk,disco and dance, spending his late teens. Falling out of the band scene in Dublin, Charkz started missing performing. Inspired by Daft Punk’s album Random Access Memories, he fell in love with DJing, mixing and disco house. Starting with small private events, and gigs in college. Charkz now is starting to make a name for himself in the club scene in Dublin, and soon Ireland.
